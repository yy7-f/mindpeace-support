Privacy Policy

MindPeace: Meditation respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, specifically regarding our use of third-party services for advertising and analytics.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email address (if you choose to create an account).
  • Meditation Activity: Session history, progress, and mindfulness data.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect identifiers such as the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) and IDFV to support advertising and analytics.
  • Usage Data: Information about ad views, clicks, and how you interact with app features.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data (e.g., app launch time) to ensure stability.
  • Coarse Location: We use your IP address to estimate a general location (e.g., city or region) for relevant advertising.

Third-Party Services & Advertising

We partner with the following third-party providers who may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob uses device identifiers and coarse location to show ads. If you grant permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt, these identifiers are used to provide personalized ads.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, cloud storage, and app analytics. Firebase helps us understand app usage and fix technical crashes.

Data Tracking & Your Choices

When you first open the app, you will see a request to allow tracking. If you select "Ask App Not to Track":

  • We will not access your IDFA.
  • You will still see ads, but they will be "contextual" (based on app content) rather than personalized to your interests.

You can change these permissions at any time in your iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. While we strive for 100% security, no electronic transmission or storage method is perfectly secure.
